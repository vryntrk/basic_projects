GENRE
High-stakes Arcade & Survival Strategy

SUMMARY
You are the King's former food taster. Once you made a mistake and the King fell ill. Then, they punished you. They gave you 5 apples, 5 pears, 5 bananas and only 3 glasses of antidote. Now, you are sitting at the table. If you can eat 5 apples, 5 pears and 5 bananas they will forgive you. Do not forget that one of the fruits might be poisonous. In short, your life is in your hands. Can you save your life?

HIGHLIGHTS
Resource Management: You have to eat 15 fruits, but you have just 3 antidotes. So, you must be lucky when you are deciding which fruit to eat.
Short Time Interval: You have to eat a fruit in 5 seconds, or you die. Also, if you are poisoned you have just 1 second to drink the antidote, or you die.
Eating Lock System: You cannot eat fruit while being poisoned.
Russian Roulette Mechanics: Every turn, poisonous fruit is randomly selected. Also, some turns all fruits might be safe.
Dynamic Poisonous Fruit Ratio: At the beginning, poisonous fruit ratio is 75%. As you consume the fruits the ratio changes.
