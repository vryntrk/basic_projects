GENRE
Survival Arcade

SUMMARY
You are a little shark living in the deep waters. Don't worry, there is plenty of food to get bigger. However, you have to be careful because there are bombs among the food. Luckily, you have 3 lives. Yet, don't forget: the more bombs you eat, the rarer hearts become. Let's try to be the king of the ocean.

HIGHLIGHTS
Growth Mechanism: Every 750 points, the shark physically grows in size.
Life and Risk Management: Dodge the bombs and collect the hearts to survive.
Dynamic Difficulty: As you play, bombs appear more frequently while hearts appear more rarely.
Timed Objects: Food, bombs and  hearts disappear 5 seconds after appearing. You have to be fast.
