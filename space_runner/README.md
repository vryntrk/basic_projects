GENRE
Top-down RPG & Strategy

SUMMARY
You are an alien and late for work, but your spacecraft has run out of power. You must collect coins to buy 5 new batteries. Also, be aware of the crazy drivers because they get faster as time passes. You lose all of your money and teleport back to the start, if they hit you. Hurry up, the boss is really mad.

HIGHLIGHTS
Economic Difficulty: Battery prices increase with every purchase.
Interactive World: You can interact with your spacecraft and the shop.
Crash Limit: 10 crashes mean the game is over. You must be quick and careful at the same time.
Directional Movement: The character rotates to face the direction of movement.
